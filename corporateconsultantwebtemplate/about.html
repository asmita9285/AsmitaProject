  • How I am surviving as an International Relations Student

    Currently, I am a third year student in a Tokyo International University situated in the middle of the Tokyo. My major is International Relations. When you are an IR student you need to focus on what your interest relies on and focus on that. First, start with studying Liberal Arts course so that you will have a basic idea of what you are studying, then walk your way up towards the IR courses and make sure you need understand some of the key terms prior to the class. Point to be notes: dont take everything too lightly and start preparing in advance.

  • Credit Criteria

    You need to obtain certain amount of credit in order to graduate. The total number of credit should be 124 where each course holds 4 credits. You can choose what you want to study considering your interests and credits in relation to your major.
